Default carousel

By default, Logo Carousel was designed to show several rows or columns of logos at once moving at different speeds to achieve that amazing effect seen on Skillshare.com. To achieve this, there is no need to have hundreds of clients or partners to put on your carousel, as Logo Carousel will automatically repeat and shuffle your available logos, even if you only have three or four of them only.

The default options include 4 rows, transparent background and right-to-left horizontal direction. All of these options and more can be changed directly from javascript code or from the html itself. We will see these 2 posibilities in another examples.
